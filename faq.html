Frequently Asked Questions

1. Which countries do you provide study visa support for?

We provide support for the USA, UK, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Germany, and many more.

2. How long does the visa process take?

It depends on the country, but typically between 2 to 6 weeks after submission.

3. Do you assist with university admission as well?

Yes, we guide you through the entire admission process including documentation and SOPs.

4. Are there any hidden charges?

No, we believe in transparency. All charges are clearly explained before starting the process.

5. Can I work while studying abroad?

Yes, most countries allow part-time work. We also help you understand their legal limits and options.
